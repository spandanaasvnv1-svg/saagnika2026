Welcome

About The Fest

SAAGNIKA–2026 stands as a distinguished platform where tradition meets talent, fostering unity and camaraderie among hostel communities across Bengaluru.

Organised under the auspices of the Spandana Students' Association, this annual celebration brings together students from diverse backgrounds to compete, collaborate, and celebrate the spirit of healthy competition.

Through a carefully curated selection of cultural performances, intellectual contests, and sporting events, SAAGNIKA embodies the values of discipline, sportsmanship, and excellence that define our institution's legacy.
